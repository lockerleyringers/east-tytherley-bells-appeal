East Tytherley Bells Appeal

The magnificent bells of East Tytherley Church have rung out since 1897 marking the most poigniant times in our lives - from weddings, christening and funerals, to the Queens Diamond Jubilee and the Kings Coronation.

After more than a century of faithful service, our bells are now in urgent need of restoration. The bell fittings have become severely worn, making them difficult to ring, if something isn't done soon our bells will soon fall silent.
